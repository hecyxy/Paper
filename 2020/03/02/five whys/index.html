5 whys

发表于 2020-03-02 | 分类于 方法论 | 阅读数

当我们在生活中、工作中，遇到一些麻烦事；5-whys准则会帮助我们更好的理解这些问题的本质和根源；另一个好处是：对于有些同学，不善于发现问题、不在行找问题，这种思考方式也有利于你想的更多、思考的更深刻；

1. The orgin of the 5 whys

The 5 whys technique was developed and fine-tuned within the Toyota Motor Coporation as a critical component of its problem-solving traning.By repeating why five times,the nature of the problem as well as its solution becomes clear.

Here’s an example Toyota offers of a potential 5 whys that might be used at one of their plants.

  1. why did the robot stop?
    • the circuit has overloaded,causing a fuse to blow.
  2. why is the circuit overloaded?
  • there was insufficient lubrication on the bearings,so they locke up.
  1. why was there insufficient lubrication on the bearing?
  • the oil pump on the robot is not circulating sufficient oil
  1. why is the pump not circulating sufficient oil?
  • the pump intake is clogged with metal shavings.
  1. why is the intake clogged with metal shavings?
  • because there is no filter on the pump.

Today,the method is used far beyond Toyota,and it’s particularly popular in the world of lean development.

2. how the 5 whys process works

five whys involves holding meetings immediately following the resolution of problems the company is facing.these problems can be anything:development mistake,site outrages(停电),marketing program failures,or even internal missed schedules.Any time unexpected happens,we could do some root cause analysis.

if somethis unexpected happened,we’ll conduct 5 whys and learn from it.

3. the 5 main steps to the 5 whys

  1. invite all affected
  2. select a meeting leader
  3. ask why five times
  4. assign responsibilities for solutions
  5. email your team the results.
why we should keep the results transparent for the team?

if the analysis is airtight,it makes it pretty easy for everyone to understand why the team is taking some time out to invest in problem prevention instead of new features.

4. real-life 5 whys examples

4.1 a example in software
  1. why did we go down? answer: because the database became locked.
  2. why did it become locked? answer:because there were too many db writes;
  3. why were we doing too many databases writes? anser:because this was not foreseen and it wasn’t load tested.
  4. why wasn’t the change load tested?answer:because we don’t have a development process set up for when we should load test changes.
  5. why don’t we have a development process for when to load test?answer:we’ve never done too much load testing and are hitting new levels of scale.

Corrective action:

  1. investigate with mongostat the database locking.
  2. stop the digest worker and kill all db operations
  3. sb work on when it makes sense to load test.
  4. educate and learn more about working in a high scale environment
2. the 5 whys in daily life
Why do i want to do this?
  • why do i want do this?it will allow me to have greater control over how i spend my time
  • i can have more flexibility in my life
  • i will be able to work from home
  • i will be able to spend more time with my son or daguhter
  • supporting my family is the most important thing to me

上面讨论的多问5个为什么，不仅可以在生活中工作中用于解决问题，对于个人可以更深刻的认识问题，了解问题的本质、根源。
